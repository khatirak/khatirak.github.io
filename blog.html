Abu Dhabi - life as an International student

Paris - Spring'24 study abroad

This was my first semester abroad in my sophomore year and it was in NYU Paris. I had the chance to stay in Abu Dhabi or go to Paris. Despite me living in the UK, I had never gone to Europe and it looked like a new amazing experience so I applied and was accepted. At first, I was quite comfortable with going but soon realised that it was very different to my NYU AD experience. I was not living on campus and I had to cook, get groceries, properly budget which was not a concern in AD. It was also my first time living in a non-English speaking country
